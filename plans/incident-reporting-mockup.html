💬 Other other Stone Anything not covered above

Design decisions:

  • Report button is subtle (not alarming) — red outline, not filled
  • Bookings with incidents get a red border + alert bar
  • Category grid selector instead of dropdown — more visible, faster selection
  • Evidence is optional — text-only reports are valid
  • Both parties see all reports — no hidden reports (transparency)
  • No resolution/status workflow — that's #361 (dispute mediation)
  • Notification uses red accent to stand out from regular notifications
