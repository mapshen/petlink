Design decisions in this catalog:

  • Puppy/Kitten Care replaces the existing puppy_rate_cents on services — moved to the add-on system so all extras live in one place
  • Full Grooming replaces grooming_addon_fee_cents — same rationale
  • Pickup & Drop-off replaces pickup_dropoff_fee_cents
  • Additional pets stays on the services table (additional_pet_price) — it's per-service pricing, not an add-on
  • Holiday rate stays on services table — it's a rate override, not an add-on
  • "Offer free" toggle available on grooming add-ons and photo updates (sets price_cents to 0)
  • 12 catalog items total — enough variety without overwhelming
