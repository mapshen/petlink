✨ What changed from v1

  • No top-level species toggle — species are added/removed via cards directly
  • Home/env moved into each card — yard/fenced in dog card, own pets per species
  • Meet & Greet per species — inside each card's services section
  • "Add another species" button — shows remaining species to add
  • "Remove" button — on each card header to stop offering that species
  • Species-specific sizes — dogs: 0–25/25–50/50–100/100+ lbs, cats: 0–8/8–12/12–18 lbs
  • Own pets per species — "I have my own dogs: 2 Labradors" / "I have my own cats: 1 indoor"

📊 What's global vs per-species now

Per-species (in card):
  • Years of experience
  • Pet sizes accepted
  • Skills & certifications
  • Max pets capacity
  • Services & pricing
  • Meet & greet pricing
  • Environment (yard, own pets)
Global (on user profile):
  • Bio / avatar
  • Home type
  • Location / service radius
  • House rules
  • Emergency procedures
  • Insurance
  • Cancellation policy
