✨ How it works

  • Toggle a pet type → species card appears/disappears instantly
  • Click a card header → collapse/expand that species section
  • Each card is self-contained → experience, sizes, skills, services+pricing all in one place

  • New species → card appears with empty fields, ready to configure
  • Header summary → shows "10 yrs · 5 services · S/M/L" even when collapsed
